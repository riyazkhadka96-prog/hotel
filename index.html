Eco-Luxury Commitments

Sustaining the Coastline

We believe that true luxury is sustainable. At Aurelian Grand, we operate with a carbon-neutral roadmap, ensuring our pristine cliffside setting is preserved for generations to come.

☀️ Solar Autonomy

65% of resort power is generated through integrated solar panels.

💧 Desalination Plant

Rooftop pool water is sustainably filtered using zero-waste desalination loops.

🥗 Zero Food Miles

Fruits, herbs, and vegetables are sourced directly from our botanical garden.

Plastic-Free Policy

Completely plastic-free guest items, employing bamboo and compostable composites.

Direct Impact (2025)

  • 🌲 10,000+ local olive trees planted along the cliff sides.
  • 🔋 120 metric tons of carbon offsets registered this fiscal year.
  • 🧴 Eco-organic partnership with local spa compounders.
  • 🌊 100% wastewater purified and recycled for botanical irrigation.
