Grand Heritage

About Aurelian Grand

Evolving classical Mediterranean architecture with premium hospitality services and zero-waste sustainability practices since 2001.

Our Philosophy

Luxury with Conscience

Aurelian Grand was founded on a simple principle: to prove that ultra-luxury hospitality can coexist in complete harmony with local environments. Over the past 25 years, we have converted our coastal cliffside into an eco-autonomous sanctuary.

Under family management, every single detail is hand-selected. From organic linens woven in northern Italy, to solar networks providing climate control to our pool, we deliver privacy without compromise.

Chronology

Resort Milestones

A timeline of award-winning hospitality accomplishments along the Monaco coastline.

2001

Groundbreaking & Laying the Foundation

Erecting the primary structural frame of Aurelian Grand on an abandoned cliffside harbor, employing local Monaco masonry.

2006

Grand Opening Celebration

Officially welcoming travellers. Opening our initial 100 suites, L'Aureole restaurant, and helicopter transport pads.

2015

Eco-Luxury Transition Complete

Replacing heating boilers with deep-earth geothermal channels. Integrating solar arrays to supply power to the resort pools.

2024

Michelin Star & Global Forbes 5-Star

L'Aureole is awarded a Michelin Guide star. The resort logs its 5th consecutive Forbes Five-Star hospitality rating.
