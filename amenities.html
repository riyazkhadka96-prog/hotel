World-Class Facilities

Luxury Resort Amenities

Every space is engineered to provide serenity, performance, and indulgence. Carefully tailored experiences to fit your private schedule.

Royal Wellness Spa

Operated in partnership with Bvlgari, our spa features private treatment villas, oxygen inhalation rooms, hydrotherapy circuits, and holistic salt pools.

⏱️ Open Daily: 08:00 — 22:00 💆 Treatments: Hot stone, seaweed wrap, thermal therapy 🧴 Products: Organic botanical oils

Rooftop Infinity Pool

Swim literally between the clouds in our heated, glass-walled rooftop infinity pool, featuring floating lounges and custom pool butler beverage service.

⏱️ Open Daily: 06:00 — 23:00 🌡️ Temperature: Climate controlled to 28°C 🍸 Bar: Cabana lounge service

Technogym Fitness Centre

Keep at your absolute physical peak utilizing our panoramic gym layout, featuring Technogym cardio systems, free weight pits, and private personal trainers.

⏱️ Open: 24 Hours / 7 Days 🏋️ Equipment: Technogym smart consoles 🧘 Classes: Morning yoga, cliffside Pilates

Executive Conference Hall

Host elite board meetings and corporate press events in our fully equipped spaces, featuring 4K projections, encryption routers, and dining menus.

👥 Capacity: Up to 150 delegates 💻 Technology: Smart conferencing, translation docks 🍽️ Catering: Degustation lunch menus available

VIP Airport Shuttle

Enjoy a seamless transition from the terminal gate. We offer private transfers in our custom fleet of Rolls-Royce Phantoms and high-capacity luxury sprinters.

🛫 Gate Pick-up: Meet & greet concierge escorts 🚗 Fleet: Rolls-Royce, Bentley, high-top sprinter 🚁 Upgrade: Heliport transfer directly to Monaco pad

24/7 Gastronomic Room Service

Dine in the ultimate privacy of your terrace balcony. Our full dining menu is delivered by tray service to your suite at any hour of the day or night.

⏱️ Available: 24/7 🍷 Menu: Fine dining selections + custom champagne carts 🛎️ Request: Digital tablet ordering in room
