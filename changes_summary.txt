========================================================================
AURELIAN GRAND - LUXURY HOTEL & RESORT WEBSITE OVERHAUL
CHANGES SUMMARY
========================================================================

A complete audit and subsequent total overhaul of the Aurelian Grand website 
has been performed. The platform has been transformed from a single-page 
landing site into an international-level, conversion-optimized, fully 
responsive multi-page luxury booking portal.

Below is the comprehensive list of modifications, architectural updates, 
and new pages implemented across the codebase:

------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. GLOBAL STYLE & ARCHITECTURE SYSTEM (style.css)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Luxury Branding & Design System: Implemented HSL-based style variables 
  accentuating rich golds (#c9a96e), deep onyx backgrounds (#0a0a0a / #121212), 
  and clean, high-contrast off-whites (#f8f5f0).
* Typography Upgrade: Integrated Google Fonts ("Playfair Display" for headings 
  and serif highlights, "Poppins" & "Inter" for clean, highly readable body copy).
* Aesthetics & Motion: Crafted custom glassmorphic cards, gold border highlights, 
  sleek shadow glows, smooth page transitions, and subtle hover scale expansions.
* Navigation Menu Overhaul: Reconfigured the main navigation dropdown layout. 
  Replaced the wide mega-menu style with a centered, compact 280px absolute 
  dropdown containing smooth fade/slide transitions on hover, ensuring a 
  balanced, premium aesthetic without intrusive screen blocking.
* Form Styling: Designed matching premium UI styles for inputs, datepickers, 
  dropdown selections, and booking checklists.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. GLOBAL SCRIPT & INTERACTION ENGINE (script.js)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Sticky Navbar Control: Monitors scrolling to trigger opacity changes and gold 
  accent lines when the user scrolls past the hero banner.
* Mobile Navigation Drawer: Accessible slide-out menu drawer that opens smoothly 
  from the side on mobile screens, complete with overlay dimming.
* Testimonials Slider: Built a custom interactive, auto-rotating testimonial 
  carousel with grab-to-slide cursor support and active gold indicator dots.
* Room Live Filter & Comparison: Interactive page logic that manages the room catalog 
  filters (by category, price, views) and highlights comparative specifications 
  side-by-side.
* Booking Invoice Engine: Processes date validation, room rates, package add-ons, 
  applies promo code discounts (e.g., "LUXURY20" for 20% off), calculates a 
  10% service charge, adds 13% VAT, and prints out a real-time invoice statement 
  formatted dynamically in Nepali Rupees (NPR).
* Urgent Notification Prompt: Periodically showcases sliding micro-popup alerts 
  ("Only 2 Suites remaining for this weekend", etc.) to prompt conversion.
* FAQ Accordion Controller: Allows smooth toggles and height transitions for 
  nested accordion questions.
* Virtual 360-Degree Panoramic Tour Simulator: Utilizes CSS translation properties 
  to slide room images horizontally, simulating a panoramic look.
* Travel Blog Sightseeing Calculator: Dynamically highlights local travel times 
  and walking ranges when clicking on attraction nodes.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. HOME PAGE (index.html)
------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Audited and restructured to serve as the core luxury entry point.
* Custom Hero Section: Visual intro with grand typography and a call-to-action to 
  the booking engine.
* Why Choose Us Grid: Highlights value props (luxury suites, central location, 
  fine dining, safety, dedicated concierge).
* Key Statistics Bar: Counts of served guests, rooms, staff count, and legacy years.
* Awards Showcase: Carousel/grid highlighting prestigious hospitality badges.
* Partner Brand Marquee: Smooth infinite scrolling marquee featuring high-end brand 
  logos and press quotes.
* Sustainability Commitment: Details regarding eco-luxury, organic dining, and 
  green energy offsets.
* Newsletter Block: Glassmorphic email capture card with instant feedback alerts.
* Instagram Grid: Hover zoom gallery of high-end hotel lifestyle visuals.
* StayNEP Concierge: Bottom-right chat popup simulation acting as a digital assistant.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. NEW INDIVIDUAL PAGES
------------------------------------------------------------------------
* rooms.html (Rooms & Suites Showcase):
  - Displays Deluxe King, Grand Suite, and Royal Penthouse room cards.
  - Curated prices matching Nepali Rupees (NPR) conversion rates ($1 = NPR 135).
  - Side-by-side room comparison matrix evaluating space, views, butler service, 
    jacuzzi availability, and private chef services.
* dining.html (Gastronomy):
  - Exhibits fine-dining restaurants (L'Aureole, Le Crépuscule, The Amber Grill).
  - Tabbed menus displaying detailed gourmet breakfast, dinner, and beverage options.
  - Custom Table Reservation Request form.
* amenities.html (Wellness & Experience):
  - Highlights Bvlgari Spa, Infinity Rooftop Pool, Fitness Hub, and VIP Transfers.
* offers.html (Special Packages):
  - Curates exclusive deals: Honeymoon Package, Family Sanctuary, Weekend Escape, 
    and Corporate Executive.
  - Outlines exact inclusions (shuttles, spa vouchers, credits) and direct booking links.
* events.html (Weddings & Summits):
  - Showcases grand ballrooms and boardrooms with capacity metrics.
  - Request for Proposal (RFP) wedding/event enquiry form.
* tour.html (Virtual Experience):
  - Houses the 360-degree virtual room scanner simulation.
  - Embedded luxury video player mockup showing resort B-roll footage.
* blog.html (Travel & Local Excursions):
  - Curates local travel articles (Heritage walks, Himalayan viewpoints, monasteries).
  - Interactive Attractions Travel Guide mapping distance and transit times.
* about.html (History & Team):
  - Details the resort timeline since 2001.
  - Showcases management cards for General Manager, Executive Chef, and Head Concierge.
* contact.html (Contact & Help Desk):
  - Address info, interactive contact form, and searchable FAQ Accordion.
* booking.html (Dedicated Booking Engine):
  - Integrates the multi-step checkout widget where guests select dates, rooms, 
    packages, input codes, and view complete billing statements before booking.

------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. SEO, METADATA & ACCESSIBILITY
------------------------------------------------------------------------
* Robots & Sitemap Setup: Created robots.txt and sitemap.xml to index all 11 pages.
* Meta Tags: Outfitted each individual page with matching unique titles, meta descriptions, 
  and Open Graph attributes.
* JSON-LD Schema: Embedded a detailed Hotel Structured Data JSON script in index.html for 
  enhanced rich snippets on search results.
* Accessibility Enhancements: Semantic HTML structures (nav, main, header, footer, 
  article, section), ARIA labels, form placeholder labels, and high contrast colors.

========================================================================
All updates have been validated for responsive mobile layouts down to 320px 
and tested for functional script interactions.
